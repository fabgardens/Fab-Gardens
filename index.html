About Fab Gardens

üåª Who We Serve

üåª Our Mission

Fab Gardens restores neglected pavements, footpaths, and public spaces where overgrowth and neglect have made walking unsafe.

We focus on practical, hands-on clean-ups that remove real hazards and help communities feel safer using the spaces they rely on every day.

This work isn't about appearances or entertainment.

It's about responsibility, dignity, and making sure public paths are safe to use again.

Why It Matters

When public paths are left overgrown or obstructed, people are forced into roads, pushed onto dangerous ground, or avoid walking altogether. That affects daily life ‚Äî especially for older residents, families, and anyone with mobility challenges.
