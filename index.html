About Fab Gardens

We're passionate about making our public spaces cleaner, safer, and more enjoyable for everyone.

Our Mission

Fab Gardens is a community-focused project that tackles neglected public areas. From overgrown footpaths to blocked pavements, we roll up our sleeves and get the job done.

We record every cleaning job from start to finish and share the transformation on our YouTube channel. Our goal is to generate awareness and encourage people across the community to nominate areas that need attention.

How We Work

When you nominate an area, we assess it, plan the clean-up, and document the entire process. You'll be able to watch the before-and-after transformation on our YouTube channel!
