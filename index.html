About Fab Gardens

Practical hands-on clean-ups helping residents in Ashtead, Leatherhead, Epsom, Reigate and surrounding Surrey areas.

🌻 Who We Serve

We serve adults aged 35–65+ who care about community safety and dignity. Our work isn't about aesthetics — it's about removing real hazards so people can walk safely again.

🌻 Our Mission

Fab Gardens restores neglected pavements, footpaths, and public spaces where overgrowth and neglect have made walking unsafe.

Why It Matters

When public paths are left overgrown, people are forced into roads or avoid walking altogether. That affects daily life — especially for older residents and families.

What We Clean

We tackle public spaces that have become dangerous or inaccessible.

🚶

Footpaths

When overgrowth blocks safe walking routes

Clearing dangerous vegetation and debris from walking paths.

🧱

Blocked Pavements

When overgrowth forces pedestrians into traffic

Removing obstructions that push people into dangerous roads.

Road Sign Visibility

When moss hides crucial safety information

Restoring visibility to road signs obscured by moss or dirt.

🌉

Footpath Bridges

When crossing becomes hazardous

Maintaining bridges along walking routes to prevent slips.

🌻

Other Public Areas

Any space with clear safety issues

Any neglected public space that poses a safety hazard.
