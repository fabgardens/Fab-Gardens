About Fab Gardens

Practical, hands-on clean-ups that make a real difference.

üåª Our Mission

Fab Gardens restores neglected pavements, footpaths, and public spaces where overgrowth and neglect have made walking unsafe.

We focus on practical, hands-on clean-ups that remove real hazards and help communities feel safer using the spaces they rely on every day.

This work isn't about appearances or entertainment.

It's about responsibility, dignity, and making sure public paths are safe to use again.

How It Works

The nomination process is simple and helps us prioritise dangerous areas.

1

You Nominate

Fill out our form with details and photos of a dangerous or neglected area.

2

We Assess

Fabio reviews the nomination and plans the clean-up operation.

3

We Clean

The work is carried out, documenting the entire process on camera.

4

We Share

The transformation video is posted to YouTube for everyone to see!

üì∏ How to Share Photos

Please upload your photo(s) to one of these free services and paste the link below:

  1. Postimages (easiest - no account needed): Go to postimages.org, click "Choose images", upload your photo, copy the "Direct link"
  2. ImgBB (no account needed): Go to imgbb.com, click "Start uploading", upload photo, copy the link
  3. Google Drive: Upload photo, right-click, click "Share", click "Copy link"
We need at least one photo to assess the area and its hazards.
